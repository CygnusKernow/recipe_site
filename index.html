Chocolate Cake

" This easy-to-make chocolate cake is a classic. It is dark, moist, rich and only one bowl to wash. Enjoy! "

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy-To-Make 10 min 30-35 min 40-45 min

Ingredients

Dark chocolate: 200g

Butter: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Flour: 80g

Eggs: 4

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven
